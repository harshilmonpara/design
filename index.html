General Info.

Jacquard design in textiles refers to a technique that uses a Jacquard loom to create intricate patterns and designs on fabric. It typically involves the use of punched cards or digital technology to control the weaving process.

Jacquard and sketchers Jacquard designers and sketchers often use specialized design software such as Adobe Photoshop or Illustrator for creating patterns and designs. They may also use CAD (Computer-Aided Design) software tailored to textile design.

A Jacquard designer in the fashion industry is responsible for creating unique and intricate textile patterns that can be used in clothing, accessories, and upholstery. They work closely with fashion designers to bring their creative visions to life.
